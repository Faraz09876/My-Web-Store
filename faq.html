Order and Delivery


This section has all information regarding ordering process and delivery details


How to process an order?


To process an order, one has to choose a product from different sections and sizes listed on the website that can be easily navigated. Once, a product or multiple products is/are selected on its/their specific page/s, click on add to cart to move forward. Once, you have selected the product/s you want to purchase, click on the checkout button. This will lead you to another tab where you need to fill out the necessary information (E-mail, Contact Information, and Shipping Address) to proceed. Then, you will receive a confirmation of your order request through e-mail and text message. Lastly, our representative will contact you in 48 hours to confirm your order and proceed to ship

What is the average delivery time?


The average delivery time is 05-07 days. Although it may take more or less time in case of time-specific circumstances

How to check the status of delivery?


The status of delivery can be tracked by our customers through the order ID provided at the time of order placement.

What are the shipping charges?


We charge a flat 300/- PKR shipping rate per order.

Are there any places where the delivery is restricted?


Yes, military protected locations are places where deliveries cannot be made due to certain military protocols. All restricted measures cannot be published in this article hence, we encourage you to check local customs for this purpose.

Payment


This section has all information regarding payment methods and procedures


How to pay cash on delivery (COD)?


To pay cash on delivery, once your chosen product is added to the cart, click on proceed to check-out after which you can fill in the shipping method and billing information. Whilst providing your payment method, please select Cash on Delivery. You will be asked for payment with the receipt alongside your purchase during the delivery the product

Are multiple orders delivered at the same time?


Yes, multiple orders are delivered at the same time as we try to formulate only a single package and the charges of shipment are then varied based on the weight of that package.

What are the available payment methods?


The available payment methods are: Credit/Debit Card Online Payment COD (Cash on Delivery)
